Custom Neoepitopes

Patient-specific peptides from ranked candidates, formatted for Class I/II immunogenicity assays (RUO).

Inputs

• Ranked candidate list (CSV/TSV)
• Peptide sequence(s) or genomic coords
• Optional: HLA types, desired lengths, purity tier

Design Options

• Length: 8–11mers (Class I), 13–25mers (Class II)
• Pooling strategy (per-antigen / per-patient)
• Modifications (N-terminal acetylation, etc.)
• Purity: 70% / 80% / 90% / >95%

QC & Deliverables

• LC-MS/HPLC purity report (per peptide)
• Lyophilized individual vials or pooled sets
• Composition sheet & handling notes

Lead Time

Standard: 2–3 weeks. Large sets or high-purity may extend timelines.

Typical Use

ELISpot/ICS, tetramer generation, T-cell priming, validation screens.
